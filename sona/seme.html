Content Words

Word Definition Writing
sijelo
sijelo		 body, torso Like a wide chest: flat line on top, three lines to bottom from left, right, center
lawa
lawa		 head, mind, brain, control, plan, govern, lead A head wearing a hat, bill facing to the right
sinpin
sinpin		 front, face, wall The right half of a box with a dot to the right. Faces towards new writing!
luka
luka		 hand, arm, tactile limb Hand outline with thumb and extended fingers
noka
noka		 foot, leg, locomotive limb Foot facing to the right
selo
selo		 boundary, skin, bark, peel, outer layer A table shape, but with four straight legs in two pairs nearer to the edges.
uta
uta		 mouth, lips, eating orifice An open, smiling mouth with a dot beneath
lupa
lupa		 hole, door, window, orifice A deep hole, curved at the bottom
monsi
monsi		 back, behind, rear The left half of a box with a dot on the left. Faces towards old writing!
nena
nena		 bump, lump, extrusion, hill, mountain, nose A tall hill
insa
insa		 inside, center, between, organ The bottom half of a box with a dot in the center
ijo
ijo		 thing Just a circle. A perfectly generic object.
