mi (me)

toki a, jan ale o! mi jan [ken.kalama.nasa][mute:]! Hey everyone, I'm gregdan3 (jan Kekan San). I speak Toki Pona, a constructed language with no words at all fewer words than you have bones! You'll find me all over the community- if you spot me, say hi! You can join too:

  • ma pona pi toki pona is the big community server!
  • kama sona is a great place to learn!
  • ma toki pona VR is the VRChat community, and my personal favorite!
  • ma mun is my own toki pona only server!

Toki Pona is how I met some of my best friends, and how I've met and worked with many of the most driven people I know. I want to share that excitement and experience with others, meet new people, and see new art in Toki Pona- I want to see Toki Pona grow! To that end, I write blogs, share lessons, make videos, and develop software.

This site is all about Toki Pona, and sometimes in Toki Pona too. You can tell by the title of a page!
