Monsutatesu

Home Blogzone Toki Pona

The “monsutatesu” phenomenon describes a behavior of the word monsuta. monsuta can mean both scary and afraid, which troubles interpretation.

mi monsuta

I am scary!

I am afraid!

mi monsuta e sina

I make you scary!

I make you afraid!

This is weird, right?
