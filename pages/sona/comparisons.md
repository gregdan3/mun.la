## Intro

Normal comparisons in Toki Pona involve the words `sama` and `ante`. While these are effective at making comparisons

#### comparing sizes

suli and lili

people, hands

#### comparing times

tenpo suli, tenpo jan, tenpo pi kulupu jan

lifetimes, civilizations

#### comparing distances

poka, weka

nearby, far away
